Computer Vision Technology for Star Trek Online Builds

SISTER is a powerful computer vision system that automatically recognizes equipment and traits in Star Trek Online screenshots. Using classical computer vision techniques combined with perceptual hashing and SSIM matching, SISTER can extract detailed build information from your screenshots, making it easier to analyze and share your builds with the community.

OCR-based Label Detection

Icon Slot Extraction

Intelligent Icon Matching

Combines perceptual hashing with SSIM matching for highly accurate equipment and trait identification.

Quality Detection

Automatically identifies quality overlays (Epic, Very Rare, etc.) on equipment icons.

Multi-Platform Support

Works with screenshots from PC and Console versions, handling both space and ground builds.

Extensible Design

Modular architecture allows for easy customization and integration into other tools.
