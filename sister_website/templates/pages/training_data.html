Contribute Build Screenshots for Training

Help us build SISTER's first neural network by contributing your Star Trek Online build screenshots. Your contributions will train a CNN for label detection, enabling SISTER to run as a fast web API without GPU requirements.

SISTER is an open-source project. Visit our GitHub repository to learn more about the project, download the latest release, or contribute.

Current release: v2025.05.31 | View Submission Statistics

Why We Need Your Screenshots

SISTER currently uses EasyOCR for text detection, which requires GPU acceleration to run fast enough for web applications. We're building a custom CNN to replace this dependency. Your screenshots help us train this network to:

  • Detect labels and text in STO screenshots without requiring EasyOCR
  • Enable SISTER to run as a fast web API without GPU acceleration
  • Work reliably across different UI scales and visual conditions
  • Handle both PC and Console platform variations
  • Support future web applications that use SISTER's computer vision pipeline

  4. Receive a detailed consent form via email
  5. Reply to confirm your consent

Screenshot Guidelines

For the best training data, please ensure your screenshots:

  • Are taken at full resolution (avoid downscaling or compression)
  • Show all UI elements clearly without any overlapping windows
  • Avoid tooltips or pop-up windows covering important information
  • Include complete build information for both space and ground when possible
  • Are unmodified originals (no editing, cropping, or filters)

License, Privacy & Data Usage

Your contributions are valuable to improving SISTER for the entire STO community. Here's how we handle your data and licensing:

  • Licensing: By submitting screenshots, you agree to license them under the Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0) license. This allows the SISTER project and others to use, share, and build upon your contributions while requiring attribution, non-commercial use, and sharing under the same terms.
  • Permitted Uses: Your screenshots will be used exclusively for training the label detection CNN, future computer vision research related to STO build analysis, and inclusion in our test suite for quality assurance.
  • Public Availability: Screenshots and derived datasets are stored in public GitHub repositories as part of our open data commitment, always under the CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 license.
  • License Permanence: The CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 license is irrevocable once data is distributed. This ensures stability for researchers and developers who rely on the dataset. However, you can request that we stop using your screenshots in future releases.
  • Future Withdrawal: You may request that we exclude your screenshots from future datasets or training runs. Previously published copies will remain available under the existing license terms.
  • Email Privacy: Your email address is used only for consent confirmation and communication about your submission. It will never be published, shared, or used for any other purpose.
  • Focused Usage: All data usage is strictly limited to improving Star Trek Online build analysis tools and related computer vision research within the SISTER project scope.

After submitting your screenshots and agreeing to the license, you'll receive an email to verify your submission. Your data will be processed according to these terms only after you confirm your consent.
