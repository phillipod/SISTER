Computer Vision for Star Trek Online Builds

SISTER is a powerful computer vision pipeline designed to detect and match equipment and trait icons within Star Trek Online (STO) screenshots. It automates OCR-based label detection, layout classification, icon slot extraction, and icon matching using a combination of perceptual hashing and SSIM for robust performance.
